How to play the game

Logging In

To play the game, you must be logged in. If you don't have an account, please sign up. If you already have an account, simply log in with your credentials.

Initiating a Game

You can initiate a game by challenging someone or yourself in the Player Challenges section of the game. Simply select a player to challenge and send them a game request.

Once you challenge some, a nitication will be sent to them and if they accept your challenge you will get the notification and their details will be added to your screen where you'll start sharing scores and chats. If they decline your challenge you will also be notified.

If someone has accepted your challenge but their details are not on your screen you must click the refresh button. You should also reresh if scores do not update immediately after answering questions.

Starting a Round

A round begins when the Start Game button is clicked. The first player (Player One on the left) gets to choose the category, difficulty level, and the number of questions they prefer for the round.

Playing a Round

Each round retrieves a set number of questions that will be answered by one player per round. The first round always goes to Player One. After each round, the turn switches to the other player.

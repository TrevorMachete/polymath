Who's smarter than who?

Choose from a variety of categories to test your knowledge in different fields

The Polymath quiz app offers different difficulty levels, catering to players of all knowledge levels where even the novice can build up to higher levels of knowledge.

User data privacy is paramount, ensuring that personal information is securely stored safely. No third party data sharing

The app provides user authentication, allowing sign-ups via multiple social media accounts for a seamless and personalized experience.

The inspiration for this project came from a love for trivia and a desire to create a platform where knowledge can be tested in a fun and engaging way. The journey a few weeks back as a portfolio project for ALX school. I worked diligently to bring this idea to life. You can find more about me on my respective LinkedIn, GitHub, and Twitter profiles. The entire project is open-sourced and available on GitHub for anyone interested in the behind-the-scenes of building a quiz game app.

-- Macs Machete
